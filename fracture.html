Fracture

ⓘ How to play

Attack green cells to disconnect the network. Isolated pieces turn blue. Win when the largest blue fragment exceeds the largest remaining green piece.

👆Tap any green cell to attack it. It turns red and is removed from the network.
💔After each attack, smaller detached green pieces turn blue (inactive). Only the largest green group survives.
🏆You win when the largest blue piece is bigger than the largest remaining green piece. Fewer attacks = better score.
◆Tap Hint to mark the current weak points — cells whose removal would split the network. Gold diamonds also appear on the solved grid.
